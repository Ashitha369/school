about Grad




Schools are foundational institutions that provide structured education to students,
facilitating both intellectual and social growth. They encompass various levels,
including primary, secondary, and sometimes tertiary education, tailored to different
age groups and learning stages. Schools offer diverse subjects such as mathematics, science,
languages, arts, and physical education, aiming to equip students with essential
knowledge and skills for future careers and personal development. Beyond academics,
schools instill social values, critical thinking, and problem-solving abilities,
and support extracurricular activities for holistic growth. Teachers, as key facilitators,
nurture an environment that fosters curiosity, discipline, and a lifelong love of learning.
